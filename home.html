Welcome to my travel blog!

About me

Greetings! Mike here. Welcome to my travel blog. I was born and raised in New York, and studied computer science at Brooklyn College. However, after many years of working in the field, I found myself deeply unsatisfied with staying put in the city. I wanted to see more of the world, and so I took a risk; I sold much of what I owned, quit my job, packed my bags, and headed to the closest airport to browse their last minute flights. On these trips, I documented every part of the journey, from the food I ate to the places I visited, and posted them online for supplemental income. One thing led to another, and now I am able to continue doing what I love thanks to patrons and viewers like you.

What you can find here

This blog will host a variety of content that will allow you to see in detail what I personally encountered on my trips. Trips will be documented in their entirety on my trips page with the highlights of the best and most memorable parts of the trip on my highlights page. You will also find a calendar schedule of my anticipated trips and releases on my schedule page.

How can I support you?

If you wish to support me, you are free to purchase merchandise from my online store at my webstore. You may also choose to book your own trips through my affiliate links on the side panel. However, if you do not have the means to support me financially, it is all right! Video views and engagement are just as helpful. Any little bit helps!
